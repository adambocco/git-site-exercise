Stay Informed: How can you help to prevent the spread of Coronavirus?

  • Wash your hands frequently!
    Soap and water for at least 20 seconds works best
    On the go, use hand sanitizer of at least 60% alcohol
  • Wear a mask!
    Homemade cloth masks or disposable store-bought masks work great! Masks with vents are ineffective
    N95 and professional-grade medical masks are in critical supply Save these types for healthcare workers or those most vulnerable
  • Keep a distance of at least 2 meters (6 feet) from others
  • Read the manuals, read the books

Stay Informed: How can you help to prevent the spread of Coronavirus?

  • Wash your hands frequently!

    • Wash with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
    • On the go, use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol

  • Wear a mask!

    • Homemade cloth masks or disposable store-bought masks work great!
    • Masks with vents are ineffective
    • N95 and professional-grade medical masks are in critical supply
    • Save these types for healthcare workers or those most vulnerable

  • Keep a distance of at least 2 meters (6 feet) from others

    • The virus spreads via respiratory droplets
    • Persons nearby may transmit the virus when coughing/sneezing
